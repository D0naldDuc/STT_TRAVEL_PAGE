Attractions/What to Do

Explore the breathtaking Magens Bay, hike through the historic Hassel Island, discover the vibrant Coral World Ocean Park, and take a scenic ride on the Skyride to Paradise Point for stunning views.

Hotels

Indulge in luxury at The Ritz-Carlton, enjoy a beachfront stay at Frenchman's Reef & Morning Star Marriott Beach Resort, experience local charm at Bolongo Bay Beach Resort, and relax at The Westin St. John Resort Villas.

Rental Cars

Explore the islands at your own pace with a rental car from Avis, Budget, Hertz, or Dependable Car Rental.

Restaurants

Savor delicious seafood at The Mafolie Restaurant, indulge in Caribbean flavors at Oceana Restaurant, enjoy a beachside meal at Morgans Mango, and experience local dishes at Gladys' Cafe.

Shopping

Shop for unique souvenirs at Mongoose Junction, explore the boutiques at Yacht Haven Grande, visit the local markets for handmade crafts, and find luxury brands at Havensight Mall.
